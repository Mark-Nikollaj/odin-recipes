Chicken Parm

Chicken parm so good your grandma likes it!

Delicate breaded chicken smothered in sauce and cheese

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Egg
  • Breading
  • Sauce
  • Oil
  • Cheese

Recipe instructions

  1. Take the eggs out and beat them till their nice and smooth
  2. Dip the chicken in the egg then smother in breading
  3. Get the oil nice and hot
  4. Put the chicken in the oil, flip when browned on one side
  5. Take the chicken cover it with sauce then cheese
  6. Slap her in the oven at 350 F untill cheese is nice and melted
